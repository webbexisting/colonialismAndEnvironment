Goals to Work Towards:

Bringing awareness to the real causes of climate change.

Avoiding Climate Doom: specific anxiety that the world will end very soon because of global warming.

We won't all die in 20 years, we promise, but we will suffer, some of us more than the rest. Focus on

stopping that from happening.

Making a conscious and continuous effort to take care of the people around us: friends, families, strangers.

Doing activism. Protests, sit ins, strikes, peitions, marches-- everything matters.
