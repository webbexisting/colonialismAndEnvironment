Colonialism and the Environment

Climate Change isn't arriving, it's here.

We all know it, we all feel it: the snowfall is never as heavy anymore, heatwaves are unbearable, and we hear of more and more spontaneous fires every year.

But is that all?

Because if we can feel the effects of climate change in the UK, what is it like for other countries? The ones we don't think about? Do they have it worse, and why do we not think about them?

And, lastly: who made it this way?
